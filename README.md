## 🧠 About Me

I’m someone who doesn’t just learn AI — I **experiment with it, break it, and rebuild it better**.

Currently pursuing **B.Tech in AI/ML**, I spend most of my time turning ideas into real-world projects — from data exploration to deploying working models.

What excites me most is not just building models, but understanding:

* *Why they work*
* *When they fail*

💡 I believe:
